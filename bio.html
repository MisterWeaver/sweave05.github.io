My name is Samuel Weaver. I was born in 1998 to a family of three but now have three siblings. In my immediate family, I have my mother, father, older brother, and two youngers brothers. We have only lived in Middlesex County in Massachusetts my entire life.

My mother's family is full of engineers and I initially wanted to pursue mechanical engineering as a career. However, during my senior year of highschool, my passion for computer science grew stronger. Accordingly, when I got to college, I decided to pursue computer science. During my freshman year of college, I realized that my long standing passion for finance, which was sparked by reading personal finance books, could lead to a career as well. Since then, I have decided to pursue a career in finance while focusing on how computers may be used to realize higher gains and increase worker efficiency.
